## Full description (≤4000 chars)
Looksy is a fashion magazine that shoots a cover for you every week.

Pick a vibe, and Looksy generates a cover-quality fashion look — then makes
every single piece instantly shoppable. No more screenshotting outfits and
hunting for where to buy them.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Pick a vibe — streetwear, old money, Sangeet, Diwali, Y2K, techwear,
   Contemporary Desi and more (or write your own style note).
2. Shoot the cover — get a polished, editorial fashion look in seconds.
3. Shop the look — tap any piece to buy it on Myntra, Flipkart, Ajio or Amazon,
   with a match score so you find the closest item fast.

WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT
• Cover-quality looks, not cartoons — editorial fashion photography, generated.
• Every piece is shoppable — "Cop the whole cover" opens the whole outfit at once.
• Made for India — occasions and styles tuned to how India actually dresses and shops.
• A fresh issue every week — it always feels new.
• Save your favourites and share your covers with one tap.
• Free to use.

[REAL-FACE] Upload a selfie and see YOURSELF on the cover — your face, the new look.

Looksy is free. When you buy through a shop link, the retailer may pay us a
small commission at no extra cost to you.
